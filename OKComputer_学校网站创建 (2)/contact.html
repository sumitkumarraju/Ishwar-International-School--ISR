Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about admissions and school life

What is the admission process?

+

Our admission process includes: 1) Online application submission, 2) Document verification, 3) Entrance assessment (if required), 4) Parent interaction, and 5) Fee payment and confirmation.

What are the fee structure and payment options?

+

Fee structure varies by grade level. We offer flexible payment options including quarterly, half-yearly, and annual payments. Detailed fee information is provided during the admission process.

What documents are required for admission?

+

Required documents include: Birth certificate, Previous school records (if applicable), Passport-sized photographs, Parent ID proof, and Address proof. Additional documents may be required for specific grades.

Do you provide transportation facilities?

+

Yes, we provide safe and reliable transportation services covering most areas of the city. Our buses are GPS-enabled with trained drivers and attendants for student safety.

What co-curricular activities are offered?

+

We offer a wide range of activities including sports, music, dance, art, drama, debate, robotics, and various clubs. Students can choose activities based on their interests and talents.

Is there an entrance exam for admission?

+

Entrance assessments may be required for certain grades to understand the student's academic level. The assessment is friendly and helps us provide appropriate support for the student's success.
