# Design Style Guide - Ishwar International School Website

## Design Philosophy

**Educational Excellence with Modern Warmth**: The design embodies the school's commitment to academic rigor while maintaining an approachable, nurturing atmosphere. We blend professional credibility with the warmth and energy of a learning community.

**Core Values in Design**:
- **Integrity**: Clean, honest layouts with clear information hierarchy
- **Inclusivity**: Accessible design that welcomes all families
- **Excellence**: Sophisticated typography and refined visual elements
- **Community**: Warm colors and imagery that foster connection

## Color Palette

**Primary Colors**:
- **Deep Forest Green** (#2D5A3D) - Represents growth, knowledge, stability
- **Warm Cream** (#F8F6F0) - Clean, welcoming background
- **Charcoal Gray** (#3A3A3A) - Professional text and accents

**Secondary Colors**:
- **Soft Gold** (#D4AF37) - Achievement and excellence
- **Muted Blue** (#5B7C99) - Trust and reliability
- **Light Sage** (#A8B5A0) - Harmony and balance

**Accent Colors**:
- **Coral Pink** (#E07A5F) - Energy and creativity (used sparingly)
- **Warm White** (#FEFEFE) - Clean spaces and contrast

## Typography

**Display Font**: Playfair Display (serif)
- Used for: Main headings, hero text, section titles
- Conveys: Academic tradition, elegance, authority

**Body Font**: Inter (sans-serif)
- Used for: Body text, navigation, forms
- Conveys: Modern clarity, readability, accessibility

**Font Hierarchy**:
- H1: 3.5rem (56px) - Hero headlines
- H2: 2.5rem (40px) - Section headers
- H3: 1.875rem (30px) - Subsection headers
- Body: 1rem (16px) - Main content
- Small: 0.875rem (14px) - Captions, metadata

## Visual Language

**Imagery Style**:
- Natural lighting with warm tones
- Authentic moments of learning and growth
- Diverse representation of students and faculty
- Professional quality with emotional resonance

**Iconography**:
- Minimalist line icons
- Educational symbols (books, graduation cap, lightbulb)
- Consistent stroke width and style
- Subtle and professional

**Layout Principles**:
- Generous white space for breathing room
- Grid-based alignment for visual order
- Asymmetrical balance for dynamic interest
- Content-first approach with clear hierarchy

## Visual Effects

**Used Libraries**:
1. **Anime.js**: Smooth page transitions and micro-interactions
2. **ECharts.js**: Data visualization for academic achievements
3. **Splide.js**: Image carousels for campus galleries
4. **p5.js**: Subtle background particle effects
5. **Pixi.js**: Interactive campus map overlay
6. **Matter.js**: Physics-based animations for hero section
7. **Shader-park**: Subtle background gradient animations

**Animation Strategy**:
- **Scroll Animations**: Elements fade in with subtle upward motion (16px)
- **Hover Effects**: Gentle scale (1.02x) and shadow expansion
- **Page Transitions**: Smooth fade with 300ms duration
- **Loading States**: Elegant skeleton screens with shimmer effects

**Header Effect**:
- Subtle parallax background with floating educational icons
- Gradient overlay transitioning from deep forest to warm cream
- Animated text reveal with staggered letter appearance

**Interactive Elements**:
- Buttons: Soft shadow with color transition on hover
- Cards: Lift effect with increased shadow and slight rotation
- Forms: Focus states with gentle glow and color shift
- Navigation: Smooth underline animation on hover

**Background Treatment**:
- Consistent warm cream background throughout
- Subtle texture overlay for depth
- Decorative elements: Scattered geometric shapes in brand colors
- No gradients on large backgrounds - solid colors only

## Responsive Design

**Breakpoints**:
- Mobile: 320px - 768px
- Tablet: 768px - 1024px
- Desktop: 1024px+

**Mobile-First Approach**:
- Touch-friendly interactive elements (44px minimum)
- Simplified navigation with hamburger menu
- Stacked layouts with generous spacing
- Optimized images for faster loading

## Accessibility

**Contrast Ratios**:
- Text on background: 4.5:1 minimum
- Interactive elements: 3:1 minimum
- Focus indicators: High contrast outlines

**Inclusive Design**:
- Alt text for all images
- Keyboard navigation support
- Screen reader friendly structure
- Color-blind friendly palette choices
