Our Mission

The primary aim of Ishwar International School is the evolution of the strength of character and stunning personality in the students. We believe in providing exceptional care to children while fostering each child's intellectual, social, and moral development in an academic-rich environment.

Our mission is to include self-discipline in students, and make them empathetic towards their fellow mates and surroundings. We emphasize on all-round development committed to the growth and development of each student.

Leadership Messages

Hear from our visionary leaders about their commitment to educational excellence

Mr. Anil Malik

Director

"

Education is a commitment to excellence in teaching and learning. No good life is possible without education. It endorses the intelligence of human beings, develops his skills and enables him to be industrious.

Life poses innumerable challenges and our goal in IIS is to help one to successfully brave through the rough seas of struggle, strife and hurdles. Here, a student is made to blossom intellectually, morally and spiritually.

Dr. Priya Sharma

Principal

"

At Ishwar International School, we believe that true education is a right blend of intelligence and character. We educate our students to maximize innate behavior along with positive social behavior, thus developing them as global citizens.

Our mission is to create a niche where learning will not be just a series of instructions but a passion, that goes beyond books, beyond instructions and beyond learning horizons.

Given that our students come from a very wide range of backgrounds and abilities, each one has something special within them. It is our focus to uncover that special talent and use it to build confidence and self-esteem.

Our Journey

A timeline of excellence, growth, and achievements in education

Foundation Year

2010

Ishwar International School was established with a vision to provide quality education that combines academic excellence with character development. Started with 200 students and 25 faculty members.

CBSE Affiliation

2012

Achieved CBSE affiliation, establishing our commitment to national education standards. This milestone marked our dedication to providing quality education recognized across India.

Infrastructure Expansion

2015

Expanded our campus with modern science laboratories, digital library, and sports complex. This expansion enhanced our ability to provide holistic education experiences.

Academic Excellence

2018

Achieved 100% board results with 95% students scoring above 90%. This milestone established our reputation for academic excellence and student success.

Digital Integration

2020

Successfully integrated digital learning platforms and smart classroom technology. Adapted to modern educational needs while maintaining our core values.

Present Day

2025

Today, we serve over 1200 students with 50+ faculty members, maintaining our commitment to excellence in education and holistic development.
